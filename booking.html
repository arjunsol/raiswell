How to Prepare

Get the most from your consultation with these preparation tips

📋 Before Your Consultation

  • Think about your project goals and must-haves
  • Consider your budget range (even a rough idea helps)
  • Look at inspiration photos or examples
  • Note any specific requirements or challenges
  • Prepare a list of questions you'd like to ask

📷 Helpful Documents to Have Ready

  • Property deeds or lease documents
  • Any existing plans or drawings
  • Previous planning permissions
  • Building regulations certificates
  • Photos of the project area
  • Inspiration images or sketches

Don't worry if you don't have everything - we can work with whatever information you have!
